    Requirements

    O' Level Day

    • 2 Hard brooms
    • 6 Toilet papers
    • 1 Tin of powdered detergent
    • A pack of 5 big size toilet balls
    • Ream of Papers (Nevius/ White moon or Double A_S.1 - S.3 only and ruled for S.4)
    • Yasarunah for all students in Shubah one and a Q'uran for Shubah two (Muslims only)
    • Saloon fee; 30,000/= (Natural hair) or 50,000/= (Plainted).

    • 1 Tin of powdered detergent
    • Scrubbing brush with handle/ squeezer
